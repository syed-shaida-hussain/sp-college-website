Principal's Message :
We welcome you all to this institution. This is the premier institution of higher learning. It has been serving the society for last over one century. We will make every effort to disseminate current knowledge, develop latest skills and imbibe rich values. We want to enrich your total personality so that you prove productive global citizens to work for the betterment of the humanity at large. The institution pursues vision, mission, goals and objectives. You are key stakeholders and every facility available in the college is created for you. We want you to be disciplined. We wish you to actively participate in curricular and co-curricular activities. The objective is that your core competencies are honed to face the challenges of real life. You are required to be regular and punctual to enjoy the camps life. The faculty is highly qualified and self motivated. Every teacher is your friend, philosopher and guide. Respect all and learn the valued lessons of life.
